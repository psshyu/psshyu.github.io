About

Hello, all! I'm currently pursuing a second bachelor's in Computer Science at the University of Utah. I completed my first in Philosophy (with a minor in Writing) at Washington University in St. Louis with the intention of becoming a tax lawyer, but ultimately decided against it. Now I'm back to messing with computers, which was something I'd done in my youth. I learned to design websites from scratch when I was about 9 (circa. 2000) and maintained a site throughout my high school years. C++ and C were the other languages I dabbled in aside from HTML/CSS and JavaScript.

Along with Web/Mobile Development, my interests in the field of computing include Natural Language Processing and Artifical Intelligence. Outside of computer science, I enjoy engaging in philosophical discourse, reading essays, and running around as a rogue in an RPG.
